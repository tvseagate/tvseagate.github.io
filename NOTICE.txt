* Embedded YouTube videos from various creators. Search youtube in the static directory to find them all.
* Blender Open Movies as example video content.
* Placeholders from Greenhill County Public Works in the static/placeholders directory.

All content is either licensed under a license that allows it to be used, or is used with permission. However, if you previously gave me permission to use your content and would like it removed from the default config files, please contact me and I will gladly do so.
